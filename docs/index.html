TEAM

We are an enthusiastic group of women, full of energy to accept the constant changes that characterize the IT sector. We are determined to take charge of our professional lives. Thanks to the knowledge and values that Adalab provides us with, we are ready to face this new challenge and we are In the mood for Code!

Strengths

We have chosen this change, we are willing to fight for it with lots of motivation and hope. We are creating a strong team in which cooperation and communication prevail and mutual support is key for us to keep moving forward.

Weakness

We are junior developers in process, assimilating lots of information in a short period of time. Despite the long hours of practice and personal commitment, we are aware of this just being the beginning and that we still have many hours to invest in our training and therefore, much to learn.
